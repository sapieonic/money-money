🎉 <b>New Feature: Debt Snowball Calculator</b>

We've added a powerful new tool to help you crush your debts faster! Here's what's new:

💰 <b>Debt Tracking</b>
• Add and manage all your loans (home, car, personal, etc.)
• Track principal, balance, interest rate, and EMI for each debt
• See real-time repayment progress with visual progress bars
• Support for fixed, reducing, and variable interest rates

📊 <b>Amortization Schedules</b>
• View month-by-month breakdown of every debt
• See exactly how much goes to principal vs interest each month
• Schedules update automatically when you make ad-hoc payments

⚡ <b>Ad-hoc Payments</b>
• Record lump-sum payments anytime (bonus, tax refund, etc.)
• Extra payments go directly to principal, saving you interest
• Full payment history with dates and notes

🎯 <b>Snowball & Avalanche Strategies</b>
• Snowball: Pay off smallest balances first for quick wins
• Avalanche: Target highest interest rates first to save money
• Compare both strategies side-by-side
• See projected payoff dates and total interest for each approach
• Track which debt gets paid off first, second, third...

🔗 <b>Smart Expense Linking</b>
• Debts auto-create a linked recurring expense (EMI) for your budget
• Or link an existing expense from your Recurring Expenses page
• Updates to debt EMI automatically sync to the linked expense
• Paying off a debt deactivates the linked expense
